Why Blockchain for Business is a necessity

Disputable Physical Contracts

Physical legal contracts require an intermediary and a bureaucratic approach for finalization, and the end result may be open to disputes

Region Locked Payments & Insecure Transaction Logs

For businesses operating on an international scale, payments might be problematic and region locked for clients. Platforms such as Stripe can be used however there are transaction limits and the whole system falls under third party scrutiny, not the business or the client's.

And here's how we rectify these issues

Legal Smart Contract

By using smart contracts, we take away the need for intermediaries. As the contract is stored on Blockchain, the details are public & immutable by third party.

Public & Secure Logs

The transaction logs of a business can be digitized & stored on the blockchain to prevent any manipulation. Being on the blockchain also ensures transparency

Instant Payments

By using cryptocurrency for payments, we take away any region blocks and guarantee immutably logged instant payments from anyone to everyone across the globe!

Tokenized Producs

Some businesses may even take interest in tokenizing their products as NFTs, allowing users to mint them and acquire the product in real life as well as a digitized web copy
